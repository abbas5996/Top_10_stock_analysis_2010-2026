📈 Stock Market Analysis — Top 9 Companies (2010–2026)
Project Overview
Analysis of 9 major stocks over 15 years (2010–2026) including the COVID crash and recovery, using Python and Power BI.
Companies Analyzed
AAPL | AMZN | AVGO | GOOG | GOOGL | META | MSFT | NVDA | TSLA
Tools & Technologies

Python & Pandas — Data cleaning and preprocessing
Power BI — Interactive dashboards and visual reporting
DAX — Custom measures and calculations

Dataset
Source: Kaggle — Stock Market Dataset (CSV format)
Key Findings

NVDA gave 49,000% total return since 2010 — highest of all 9 stocks
Worst single day return: -85.28% — COVID crash (2020)
Best single day return: +278.51%
TSLA is most volatile stock (risk score 26.0)
MSFT is most stable stock with consistent returns
Trading volume dropped from 192 billion (2011) to near zero by 2026
Post 2023 recovery — META showed the biggest price surge

Dashboard Pages

Executive Overview — Total companies, trading days, best stock, price journey
Stock Price Trend Analysis — Adjusted close price, trading volume over time
Returns & Volatility Analysis — Daily returns, monthly heatmap, best/worst days
Risk vs Return Comparison — Which stock gave best return vs highest risk

Main Insight
High return always comes with high risk.
NVDA and TSLA made the most — and lost the most on bad days.
MSFT made the least — and never collapsed.
Data doesn't tell you what to buy. It tells you what kind of investor you are.
